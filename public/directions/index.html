ACSL Directions & Parking

GateWay Community College - Washington Campus 108 N. 40th St. Phoenix, AZ 85034

The ACSL is located in Room 2108 of the MA building.

Park in lots 3 and 4 at Gateway Community College on 40th and Washington Street. The Advanced Cyber Systems Lab is located on the second floor of the MA building in room 2108. Go through the double glass doors and up the staircase to your immediate left. The room is the first door on the right in the hallway. The wall is bright orange so you can’t miss it. There is also a sign on the door with a number you can call for someone to come down and lead the way.

Hours

Monday thru Thursday - 4pm to 8pm
