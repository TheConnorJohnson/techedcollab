## Past Events

{{< cards link="https://www.meetup.com/hardware-happy-hour-3h-phoenix/events/315065124/" title="Hardware Happy Hour (3H) Phoenix - June 2026" >}}

The Hardware Happy Hour is a social get-together of engineers, designers, coders, and builders, both hobbyists and professionals. There are no presentations or an agenda. We just get together to talk, share projects, offer suggestions, and often learn in the process.

If you have an electronic project you'd like to show off, bring it!
(Just for the record, we talk a LOT about software too)

If you have a project that has you stumped, bring it!
(There'll be some grade-A geeks there that may be able to help)

If you don't have a project, come and hang out! Maybe you'll get inspired.
